Prototype Design Goals:

Gameplay:
-snowman roams around a neighbourhood block shooting carrots at kids that run away while parents try attack

Movement:
-Move with WASD
-Aim with mouse 360 independant of WASD
-Dash in direction of current WASD

Enimies:
-parents attack, kids run away
-Spawn at certain spots when player is x distance away (x axis value both infront and behind)
-Run towards player
-Kill player if touch
-33% body damage, 66% headshot damage
